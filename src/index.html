    PASTA ROLLER & CUTTER SET (KPRA)

    Bring the taste of fresh pasta into your home with this KitchenAid® Stand Mixer attachment set. The pasta roller helps you guide sheets of just-made dough through to the desired thickness. You can then cut the pasta yourself into thick pappardelle noodles or squares for ravioli or run the sheets through the fettuccine or spaghetti cutter for perfectly uniform pasta. This 3 piece, all metal set contains a pasta roller, fettuccine cutter and linguine cutter. Includes wooden cleaning brush.

    GOURMET PASTA PRESS (KRAV)

    Turn your kitchen into a pasta-making workshop with the pasta press that attaches directly to your KitchenAid® Stand Mixer. With six interchangeable pasta plates, you can create fresh strands of spaghetti or bucatini pastas, as well as short pastas like rigatoni, fusilli, large macaroni or small macaroni.

    RAVIOLI MAKER (KPEXTA)

    Use the power of your KitchenAid® Stand Mixer to bring the taste of fresh pasta into your home. The ravioli maker lets you manually guide two sheets of fresh pasta through the hopper, filling the pockets with whatever combination of ingredients you choose. Recommended for use with the KitchenAid® Pasta Roller attachment. Includes filling scoop and cleaning brush.

    FOOD PROCESSOR (KSM2FPA)

    Get processing with your KitchenAid® Stand Mixer. The Food Processor attachment simply attaches to the hub of your Stand Mixer to slice, dice, shred and julienne your favorite fruits, vegetables and hard cheeses. The exclusive ExactSlice™ System with 6 settings allows you to slice from thick to thin with one slide of the lever. Includes reversible shredding disc, adjustable slicing disc, julienne disc, dicing disc and blade, dicing disc cleaner and storage case.

    ROTO SLICER/SHREDDER (RVSA)

    Use the power of your KitchenAid® Stand Mixer with this versatile attachment. The slicer and shredder makes unbelievably quick work of shredding cheeses for pasta or pizza, slicing vegetables for stir fry, shredding carrots and cabbage for slaw and much more. Includes 2 slicer and 2 shredder cones.

    SPIRALIZER (KSM1APC)

    Whether looking for more ways to eat healthfully or fresh ideas for exploring culinary creativity, the new spiralizer attachment for the iconic and versatile KitchenAid® Stand Mixer is destined to become “go to” gear for adventurous home cooks. The KitchenAid® Spiralizer Attachment includes five blades and offers nine combinations of spiralizing, slicing, peeling and coring. The spiralizer features durable metal construction and a storage case for convenient organization of both the unit and its dishwasher-safe accessories.

    STRAINER & GRINDER (FVSFGA)

    This versatile tool attaches right to your KitchenAid® Stand Mixer and uses the power of its motor to strain and puree fruits and vegetables with total ease. Includes fine and coarse grinding plate and food pusher.
